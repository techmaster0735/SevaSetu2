Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a volunteer?

Simply fill out the contact form above, select "Volunteer" as your role, and tell us about your interests and availability. We'll match you with suitable opportunities.

Can my NGO partner with SevaSetu?

Yes! We welcome NGO partnerships. Select "NGO Representative" in the form and provide details about your organization and projects.

Is there any cost to join?

No, joining SevaSetu is completely free for volunteers and citizens. We believe in making social impact accessible to everyone.

How are volunteers matched with projects?

We use your skills, interests, location, and availability to match you with relevant projects and NGOs in your area.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! Many of our projects offer remote volunteering opportunities, especially in areas like digital marketing, content creation, and online tutoring.

How do I track my volunteer hours?

Once you join, you'll have access to a personal dashboard where you can track your volunteer hours, points, and achievements.
